Israeli Snacks Collection

From Israel to Korea with Love
Welcome! These are popular Israeli snacks that represent the unique flavors of Israel. Please check the allergen information carefully before trying each snack. Enjoy your taste of Israel!
🥜 Bamba
(במבה)
Israel's most beloved peanut puff snack. Light, crunchy, and melts in your mouth. This is the #1 snack in Israel!
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Peanuts
May contain: Soy
🍓 Strawberry Bamba
(במבה תות)
Sweet strawberry-flavored version of classic Bamba. A special treat popular at celebrations and parties.
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Soy
May contain: Peanuts
🔥 Bamba Marshmallow
(במבה מרשמלו)
Special Bamba filled with marshmallow cream, combining the classic peanut taste with sweet marshmallow.
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Peanuts and Soy
May contain: Tree nuts, Sesame, Gluten
🥙 Bissli Falafel
(ביסלי פלאפל)
Crunchy wheat snack with authentic falafel flavor. Experience the taste of Israeli street food in a snack!
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Wheat gluten
May contain: Soy
🍖 Bissli BBQ
(ביסלי ברביקיו)
Crunchy wheat snack with smoky barbecue flavor. Loved by kids and adults alike!
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Wheat gluten and Soy
🍫 Click Biscuit (Red Bag)
(קליק אדום)
Crispy chocolate cereal balls coated with milk chocolate. A beloved Israeli chocolate snack.
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Milk, Wheat gluten, Soy
May contain: Sesame, Hazelnuts, Pecans, Cashews, Coconut, Almonds, Peanuts
🍫 Click Chocolate Balls
(קליק כדורים)
Crispy chocolate balls with white and dark chocolate coating. Perfect for chocolate lovers!
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Milk, Wheat gluten
May contain: Sesame, Hazelnuts, Pecans, Cashews, Coconut, Almonds, Peanuts, Soy
🍫 Blondie
(בלונדי)
Premium white chocolate bar with hazelnuts. Sweet and crunchy in every bite.
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Milk, Almonds, Hazelnuts, Soy
May contain: Gluten (wheat, barley), Peanuts, Pecans, Eggs, Sesame
☕ Coffee Filled Chocolate
(שוקולד במילוי קפה)
Premium milk chocolate filled with rich instant coffee cream. Perfect combination for coffee and chocolate lovers.
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Milk, Almonds, Hazelnuts, Soy
May contain: Gluten (wheat, barley), Peanuts, Pecans, Eggs, Sesame
🍯 Halva
(חלווה)
Traditional Middle Eastern sweet made from sesame tahini with various nuts. Sweet, crumbly, and melts in your mouth.
⚠️ Allergens
Contains: Sesame, Walnuts, Cashews, Hazelnuts, Pistachios, Soy
May contain: Walnuts, Pistachios, Almonds, Cashews, Coconut, Hazelnuts
Note: May contain hard sugar pieces
